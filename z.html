⭐ What is Zephyr?

Zephyr is the official dance club of
Vishwakarma Institute of Technology.

It represents VIT Pune in inter-college dance competitions and cultural festivals.





🎯 Purpose & Focus

✔ Promote dance and performing arts
✔ Build stage confidence
✔ Represent VIT in cultural events
✔ Encourage creativity & teamwork





🛠 What Members Do

🔹 Practice choreographies regularly
🔹 Perform in college fests
🔹 Compete in inter-college events
🔹 Conduct dance workshops





🧠 Skills You Gain

1.Stage confidence

2.Team coordination

3.Creativity

4.Physical fitness
