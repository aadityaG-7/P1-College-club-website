⭐ What is Vishwa Shauryam?

Vishwa Shauryam is a leadership and defense-oriented student club at
Vishwakarma Institute of Technology.

It focuses on discipline, patriotism, personality development, and leadership skills, especially for students interested in 
defense services and nation-building roles.





🎯 Purpose & Focus

✔ Develop leadership and decision-making skills
✔ Encourage a disciplined and resilient mindset
✔ Organize sessions related to defense services preparation
✔ Promote values like courage, integrity, and teamwork





🛠 What Members Do

🔹 Attend leadership talks & motivational sessions
🔹 Participate in activities that build confidence and discipline
🔹 Engage in discussions about armed forces & national service
🔹 Work in organizing teams for events and seminars





🧠 Skills You Gain

1.Leadership

2.Public speaking

3.Confidence & discipline

4.Team coordination
