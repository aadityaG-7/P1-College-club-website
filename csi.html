⭐ What is CSI VIT Pune?

CSI VIT Pune is the official student branch of
Computer Society of India
at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology.

It is one of the major technical coding & development clubs in the college.





🎯 Purpose & Focus

✔ Promote coding, software development, and tech innovation
✔ Conduct hackathons, workshops, and competitions
✔ Build a strong developer community
✔ Provide industry exposure and networking





🛠 What Members Do

🔹 Participate in coding competitions
🔹 Attend workshops on web dev, AI, ML, etc.
🔹 Organize technical events
🔹 Work on collaborative tech projects





🏆 Why Join CSI?

✔ Improves programming skills
✔ Adds value to resume
✔ Helps in placements & internships
✔ Great for students interested in IT & software careers
