🤖 What Is TRF at VIT Pune?

TRF – The Robotics Forum is the official robotics club of VIT Pune, focused on robotics, automation, and hands-on technical learning.
It’s one of the major technical clubs at the institute where students passionate about robotics work together on projects,
competitions, and skill development.





⭐ Main Focus

TRF stands for The Robotics Forum, and its main goals are:

To build and design robots and robotic systems.

To participate in national and international robotics competitions like ABU Robocon, e-Yantra, Smart India Hackathon, and other tech
challenges.

To conduct workshops and training sessions that teach members real robotics skills — from sensors and motors to embedded systems and
computer vision.





🛠 What Members Do

As a student member of TRF, you can:

✔ Attend hands-on workshops on robotics basics and advanced topics like CAD, PCB design, and automation systems.
✔ Work on real robotics projects that help apply classroom concepts to practical problems.
✔ Develop skills in programming, mechanical design, and electronics.
✔ Compete in ABU Robocon, where robots must complete specific engineered tasks within a time limit.





👥 Who Can Join

Students from any engineering branch who are interested in robotics and automation can join TRF.

It’s great for people who want hands-on experience, teamwork, and opportunities to compete or showcase skills.





🎯 Vision & Mission

Vision:
To be a leader in innovation and excellence in robotics, and create a community passionate about advancing robotic technology.

Mission:
To provide a platform where students can learn, collaborate, build robots, improve teamwork and communication, and participate
in competitions and workshops that prepare them for careers in tech.





🏆 Achievements & Legacy

TRF has a long legacy of more than 15+ years in robotics at VIT Pune.

The team has regularly taken part in ABU Robocon and other prestigious robotics contests, often performing strongly.





🧑‍🔧 Benefits of Joining TRF

✔ Strong hands-on learning experience.
✔ Real exposure to robotics design and engineering.
✔ Teamwork and project-based skills that look great on resumes.
✔ Networking with peers interested in robotics and hardware tech.
